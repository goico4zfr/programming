Departments

Business

The Business department offers Associate programsin business-related fields such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, human resources, and international business, among others.

Computer Science

he Computer Science department offers Associates in Science degrees in programs under computer science. Computer science is a field that focuses on the study of computing technology, including computer hardware, software development, programming languages, computer networks, database systems, and more.

Humanities and Education

The Humanities and Education department focuses on the study of human culture, thought, and creativity. This department includes a range of disciplines, including literature, history, philosophy, linguistics, communication studies, and education.

Math and Science

The Math and Science department focuses on the study of mathematical and scientific principles, theories, and applications. This department offers Associate degrees in a range of disciplines, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science.

Social Science

The Social Science department focuses on the study of human behavior, relationships, and societies. This department includes a range of disciplines, including sociology, psychology, anthropology, political science, economics, and communication studies.
